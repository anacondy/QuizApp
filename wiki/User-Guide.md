# User Guide

Learn how to use QuizApp to prepare for government competitive exams.

## üéØ Taking a Quiz

### 1. Select Your Exam Category

On the home page, choose from three exam types:

- **SSC** - Staff Selection Commission
- **BANK** - Banking Sector Exams
- **RRB** - Railway Recruitment Board

### 2. Quiz Interface

Once you select a category, you'll see:

- **Question Counter**: Shows how many questions you've answered
- **Section Name**: Current question's section (e.g., "General Awareness")
- **Pagination**: Quick navigation to any question
- **Question Card**: The current question
- **Options**: Four answer choices (A, B, C, D)

### 3. Answering Questions

**Desktop:**
- Click any option to select your answer
- Use keyboard shortcuts (see below)

**Mobile:**
- Tap any option to select your answer
- Swipe or use navigation buttons

### 4. Navigation

**Keyboard Shortcuts:**
- `‚Üê` / `‚Üí` - Previous/Next question
- `A` / `D` - Scroll pagination left/right
- `Enter` - Submit quiz (when all answered)

**Pagination:**
- Click any number to jump to that question
- Blue number = current question
- Green border = answered question

### 5. Completing the Quiz

- Answer all 10 questions
- A prompt will appear: "‚úÖ All questions answered! Press ENTER to see results."
- Press Enter or wait for automatic submission

## üìä Understanding Results

### Score Summary

You'll see:
- **Final Score**: e.g., "Your Score: 7/10"
- **Accuracy Percentage**: e.g., "Accuracy: 70.0%"

### Detailed Review

For each question, you'll see:

- **Question number and text**
- **Your answer** (highlighted in red if incorrect)
- **Correct answer** (always shown)

**Color Coding:**
- üü¢ Green border = Correct answer
- üî¥ Red border = Incorrect answer

### What to Do Next

- **Play Again**: Click the button to return to category selection
- **Review Mistakes**: Study incorrect answers carefully
- **Practice More**: Try another category or retake the same quiz

Switch between exam categories anytime:

1. Look at the bottom navigation bar

3. You'll be redirected to the new category
4. Your previous quiz progress will be lost

## üéì Exam Categories Explained

### SSC (Staff Selection Commission)

**Sections:**
- General Awareness: Current affairs, history, geography
- Quantitative Aptitude: Math, arithmetic, algebra
- English Comprehension: Grammar, vocabulary
- General Intelligence & Reasoning: Logical reasoning, puzzles

**Question Format:** 10 questions per quiz, mixed sections

### BANK (Banking Sector)

**Sections:**
- Reasoning Ability: Logical reasoning, coding-decoding
- Quantitative Aptitude: Math problems, data interpretation
- English Language: Grammar, comprehension
- General / Financial Awareness: Banking, economy

**Question Format:** 10 questions per quiz, mixed sections

### RRB (Railway Recruitment Board)

**Sections:**
- Mathematics: Arithmetic, geometry, algebra
- General Intelligence and Reasoning: Analogies, patterns
- General Awareness: Railway knowledge, current affairs

**Question Format:** 10 questions per quiz, mixed sections
